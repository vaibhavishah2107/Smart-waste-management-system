About the Waste System

Our smart waste platform connects sensor-enabled bins, collection vehicles, and citizen apps. It reduces overflowing bins, cuts fuel costs through optimized routing, and improves recycling rates by giving households clear guidance. Municipal teams get dashboards and alerts; citizens get transparency and a friendly assistant for quick answers.

Segregation
Source-segregate into wet, dry, and hazardous to improve recycling.
Collection
Dynamic routes based on fill level, traffic, and service windows.
Processing
Composting for organics, MRFs for recyclables, safe e-waste hubs.

How it Works

Step 01
Sense

Ultrasonic and weight sensors measure fill and contamination levels in real time.

Step 02
Analyze

Edge + cloud analytics forecast pickups and detect anomalies.

Step 03
Optimize

Auto-generate collection routes to minimize distance and time.

Step 04
Engage

Citizens report issues and chat with an AI assistant for guidance.
